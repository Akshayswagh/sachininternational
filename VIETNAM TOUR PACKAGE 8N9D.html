Day Wise Itinerary

DAY 1: HANOI ARRIVAL – HALF CITY TOUR (D)

  • ● Upon arrival at Noi Bai International Airport, guide & driver will pick you up by private car to city center.
  • ● Hotel check-in time 14:00. Early check-in NOT included.
  • ● Afternoon, enjoy half day city tour of Hanoi: Ho Chi Minh Complex (outside only), Ba Dinh Square, Uncle Ho' Stilt House, One Pillar Pagoda, Tran Quoc Pagoda, Train Street.
  • ● Shopping at Old Quarter.
  • ● Enjoy the famous Water puppet show at local theatre.
  • ● Meals: Indian Dinner.
  • ● Overnight in Hanoi.

DAY 2: HANOI – NINH BINH – HOA LU – TRANG AN – HANOI (B/D)

  • ● Morning, transfer to Hoa Lu District, Ninh Binh Province (2.5 hour drive).
  • ● Discover the first capital of Vietnam. Visit the two old temples dedicated to Dinh Tien Hoang and Le Dynasty kings.
  • ● Afternoon, continue to visit Trang An tourist attraction. Take a boat trip to visit cave complex: Sang Cave, Toi Cave, Ba Giot Cave, Nau Ruou, seeing scenery of mountain and rivers – "Ha Long on land of Ninh Binh".
  • ● Back to Hanoi.
  • ● Meals: Breakfast + Indian Dinner.
  • ● Overnight in Hanoi.

DAY 3: HANOI – HALONG BAY O/N CRUISE (B/L/D)

  • ● Transfer to Ha Long by private car (no guide).
  • ● 12h00 – 12h30: Embark Halong Cruise, welcome drink, safety guidelines, check-in.
  • ● 13h15: Lunch while cruising in the magnificent Bay.
  • ● 16h00: Join a guided visit to Stalemate Cave.
  • ● 17h20: Return to Cruise, enjoy sunset happy hours.
  • ● 18h00: Join a Cooking Demonstration & Food Décor Class.
  • ● 19h00 – 21h00: Dinner is served on cruise.
  • ● 21h00 – 22h00: Happy hours, Squid Fishing, Karaoke or relax.
  • ● Note: Itinerary may be subject to change by Cruise brand; local authority or weather without prior notice. No Indian food on cruise, clients can have vegetarian meals instead.
  • ● Meals: Breakfast + Lunch + Dinner.
  • ● Overnight on Halong Cruise.

DAY 4: HALONG – HANOI – FLIGHT TO DA NANG (B/Br/D)

  • ● 06h30: Tai-Chi Lesson on sundeck.
  • ● 07h00 – 08h00: Breakfast.
  • ● 08h00 – 09h00: Climb to the peak of Island for panoramic views, relax on the beach, swim.
  • ● 09h00: Return to Cruise for freshening up.
  • ● 09h30: Check out cabin, continue roaming towards Bai Tho Mountain.
  • ● 10h30: Have early lunch (brunch).
  • ● 12.00: Arrive back at Ha Long city for disembarkation.
  • ● Transfer back to Hanoi by private car (no guide) to take a flight to Da Nang (no guide).
  • ● Note: The Journey of Cruise is for reference only. It may be changed depending on Cruise. No Indian food on cruise, clients can have vegetarian meals instead.
  • ● Meals: Breakfast + Brunch + Indian Dinner.
  • ● Overnight in Da Nang.

DAY 5: DANANG – SON TRA – MARBLE MOUNTAIN – HOI AN (B/D)

  • ● Morning, enjoy a half day city tour in Danang:
  • ● Linh Ung Pagoda on Son Tra Peninsula, featuring the tallest Lady Buddha Statue in Vietnam (67 meters).
  • ● Marble Mountain: Five limestone mountains with harmonious ecological beauty and spiritual life.
  • ● Non Nuoc Stone Carving Village: Admire exquisite sculptures made from stone.
  • ● Late afternoon, visit Hoi An Ancient Town – A World Cultural Heritage. Explore Japanese Bridge, Phuc Kien Communal House, Tan Ky Traditional House, Museum, Handicraftsman at work. Free time for shopping.
  • ● Back to your hotel in Danang.
  • ● Meals: Breakfast + Indian Dinner.
  • ● Overnight in Danang.

DAY 6: DA NANG – BANA HILLS – GOLDEN BRIDGE – DA NANG (B/D)

  • ● Today we visit Bana Hills by taking Cable Car (5 world scores).
  • ● Bana Hills is known as the “green lung” of Da Nang City, offering cool climate and 4 seasons in one day.
  • ● French Village: A replica of classical and romantic France.
  • ● Golden Bridge: A new Vietnamese-designed bridge, a famous landmark.
  • ● Fantasy Park: Free on your own for joining games in the third biggest indoor games zone in Vietnam (2 hours).
  • ● Meals: Breakfast + Indian Dinner.
  • ● Overnight in Da Nang.

DAY 7: DA NANG DEPARTURE (B)

  • ● Breakfast, free at leisure then transfer to Airport for flight home (NO GUIDE).
  • ● Meals: Breakfast.
  • ● END OF ALL SERVICES!!!!
