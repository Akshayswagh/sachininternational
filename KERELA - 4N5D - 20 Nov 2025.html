🗓️ Detailed Day-Wise Itinerary

Day 01: Cochin Airport to Munnar (3 to 4 hrs)

  • ● Arrive at Cochin Airport and transfer to Munnar.
  • ● Visit Rubber Plantations, Tea Plantations, Spice Plantation Tour, Cheeyappara Waterfalls, Hydel Park, Munnar town, Tea Museum etc.
  • ● Check in to the resort. At night, relax at the resort.

Day 02: Munnar Sightseeing

  • ● After breakfast visit Mattupetty dam, Kundala lake, echo point, Eravikulam National park, Rajamala hills, Rose Garden, Shopping in Munnar town.
  • ● In the evening option for ayurvedic massage, cultural programmes (Kathakali, Marital arts etc).
  • ● At night relax at the resort.

Day 03: Munnar to Thekkady (3 hrs)

  • ● After breakfast, transfer to Thekkady.
  • ● Visit Tea Garden, Lock Heart Gap, Spice Plantation Tour, visit Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary.
  • ● Option for boating organized by KTDC, Elephant ride etc.
  • ● Evening option for Kumily town shopping spices etc.
  • ● Relax at the hotel.

Day 04: Thekkady to Alleppey (4 to 5 hrs)

  • ● After breakfast checkout and transfer to Alleppey.
  • ● Visit Alleppey Beach. Post lunch check in to hotel.
  • ● In the evening may opt for backwater shikara boat ride (on additional cost).
  • ● Overnight stay in Alleppey Hotel.

Day 05: Alleppey to Cochin (1.30 to 2 hrs)

  • ● After breakfast drive to Cochin and drop at Cochin Airport for onward journey.
  • ● End of a memorable tour offering you remarkable experience and memorable memories to relish in forever.

Important Notes

  • ● Pick up & drop before 7am or after 7.30pm will incur extra charges.
  • ● Rooms are subject to Availability.
  • ● Hotel check in 2pm, checkout 10am.
  • ● Not all hotels provide Buffet dinners, some hotels have fixed menus as per hotel policy.
  • ● AC in vehicle will not be operated in the hilly region.
  • ● Please note houseboat check in time is 13.30hrs & check out time is 9.00hrs.
  • ● Houseboat sailing time will only be until 17.00hrs maximum.
  • ● In deluxe houseboats AC will operate only in the bedroom from 21.00hrs to 6.00hrs only.

🧳 Recommended to Pack

  • Light cotton clothes (Apr–Oct), light woollens (Nov–Feb).
  • Sunglasses, sunscreen, hat, mosquito repellent.
  • Camera / binoculars (especially for wildlife and houseboat trips).
  • Raincoat or umbrella (monsoon season: June–Sept).
  • Valid ID proof (Aadhaar, Passport, etc.).
